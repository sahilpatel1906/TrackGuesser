HOW TO PLAY

Starting:
  • Select a playlist or provide your own Spotify public playlist URL
  • Select a difficulty option, easy (Hear a whole 30s), normal (Hear more for each guess), or hard (Hear JUST 5s)
  • The game will select 5 random tracks from the playlist to be quizzed upon
Playing:
  • You start the round with 1000 points, upon guessing correctly you will add those points to your score
  • Listen to the song!
  • Depending on your difficulity, you'll have a different length of the preview to hear
  • Once you've listened, you have the ability to guess the artist of the track to earn bonus points. You only get 1 guess
  • After guessing the artist, you then are tasked with putting in what you think the song title is.
  • The Album Hint will reveal album name and art, at the cost of 100 points
  • The Artist Hint will reveal the artists for the track, at the cost of 200 points. If you've guessed the artist correctly, you can't use this hint!
  • The Track Hint will reveal the first letter of the track title, at the cost of 100 points.
  • Each incorrect song guess OR skip will deduct 100 points. You have a maximum of 6 guesses before "flunking out" and moving on to the next round, earning 0 points
  • The maxmimum score is 5,500 points, strive for the leaderboards!
